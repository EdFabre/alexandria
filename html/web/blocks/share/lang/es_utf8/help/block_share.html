Share with a friend

This facility will automatically open a new email using the mail client on your computer.

The body of the email will have this text automatically entered, which you may adjust and send to who you like:

"Hi, I've just come across the OpenLearn website (www.open.ac.uk/openlearn) where you can study Open University materials online for free. I particularly thought you might be interested in "[the name of the unit you are currently on]" which is available at: [a link to the unit you are currently on] Hope you find it useful!"

This facility does require JavaScript to be enabled in your browser. Also you will need a mail client on your PC (not a web based email account).

However, in the absence of either JavaScript or a local mail client, please feel free to copy and paste the permalink to the unit from the block into any email account or other correspondance or web page where a link to this unit is required.

Please note that neither The Open University nor OpenLearn receive or keep a copy of emails generated through the share with a friend facility.
